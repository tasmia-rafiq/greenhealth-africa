Organizational Strengthening

Many local organizations are mission driven and have the passion and the commitment to make a difference to improve the lives of the people in their community. Often however, they lack the capacity, organizational systems and strategies to optimally meet their mission objectives. These gaps can be in strategic planning, resource mobilization, donor program management, financial management, or a range of other things.

GreenHealth has the expertise in organizational development to help organizations build the skills, systems and strategies to better serve their communities.

Our services include:

Organizational capacity assessment

Development of organizational strategy, vision and mission

Fundraising and proposal development

Corporate Communications and Marketing

Financial systems

Monitoring and evaluation and use of data for decision-making

Program and donor management

IT data systems
