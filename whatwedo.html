What We Do

“We at Greenhealth Africa are in the business of sharing our technical and operational expertise with organizations so that they may grow in their mission to better serve their communities.”

Sustainable development in Africa can only be achieved when local organizations at the community level are empowered to generate their own local solutions to their particular needs. GreenHealth Africa helps local organizations to achieve their ambitions by strengthening their technical and organizational capacities.

We provide to our clients a fusion of international-level expertise and experience with an unwavering commitment to knowledge sharing and building technical expertise. Our core strength is the collective experience of our team across a wide range of organizations, countries, health areas, program leadership and implementation, and strengthening of organizational systems to deliver impact.
